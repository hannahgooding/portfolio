About Me

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer graduating from the Founders and Coders coding bootcamp in early July 2020. This experience has accelerated my technical knowledge, and built up my confidence with programming in a team and remote working.

My previous experience working in fashion retail and media exposed me to the wastefulness of the industry on a global scale. I was inspired to move into programming because I saw how companies are using technology to solve life-changing problems, particularly with sustainability and accessibility.

I made my first steps into coding through talking to developers at workshops and meet-ups, so now I enjoy paying it forward by coaching at codebar. I have also been teaching a beginner's web development course to job seeker's in Islington as part of a Founders and Coders outreach project which I have found very rewarding.
